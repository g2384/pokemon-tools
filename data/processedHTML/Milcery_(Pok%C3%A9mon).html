Milcery (Japanese: マホミル Mahomil) is a Fairy-type Pokémon introduced in Generation VIII.

It evolves into Alcremie while holding a Sweet when its Trainer spins and strikes a pose. The form it evolves into depends on time of day, the length of the spin, and whether the Trainer was spinning clockwise or counterclockwise. Its appearance is further determined by the type of Sweet that is held.

  • Milcery evolves into Vanilla Cream Alcremie after spinning clockwise for less than 5 seconds during the day.
  • Milcery evolves into Ruby Cream Alcremie after spinning counterclockwise for less than 5 seconds during the day.
  • Milcery evolves into Matcha Cream Alcremie after spinning clockwise for less than 5 seconds at night.
  • Milcery evolves into Mint Cream Alcremie after spinning counterclockwise for more than 5 seconds at night.
  • Milcery evolves into Lemon Cream Alcremie after spinning clockwise for more than 5 seconds at night.
  • Milcery evolves into Salted Cream Alcremie after spinning counterclockwise for less than 5 seconds at night.
  • Milcery evolves into Ruby Swirl Alcremie after spinning counterclockwise for more than 5 seconds during the day.
  • Milcery evolves into Caramel Swirl Alcremie after spinning clockwise for more than 5 seconds during the day.
  • Milcery evolves into Rainbow Swirl Alcremie after spinning counterclockwise for more than 10 seconds between 7:00 PM and 7:59 PM in-game time.

Biology

Milcery is a small cream-colored Pokémon that resembles a splash of milk. It has a droplet-shaped body composed of cream, with two arms and a crown of six protrusions. These protrusions are made out of that same cream with droplets constantly jumping from them. It has completely white eyes. Milcery is born from sweet-smelling particles in the air. It is rumored that patisseries visited by Milcery are guaranteed success and good fortune. It is a female-only species.

Main series

Major appearances

Milcery debuted in The Sweet Taste of Battle!, where one was caught by Goh. She evolved into an Alcremie almost immediately afterwards when he placed a Strawberry Sweet on her and spun her around.

Trivia

Origin

Milcery appears to be based on a drop of milk or a blob of cream.

Name origin

Milcery may be a combination of milk and sorcery, mercury, or alchemy.

Mahomil may be a combination of 魔法 mahō (magic) and milk.
