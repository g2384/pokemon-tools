Effect

In Pokémon Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, all games from Generation IV to Generation VII, and Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl, it can be given to a Move Reminder to teach one move to one Pokémon.

In Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl, after using 10 Heart Scales total, the cost is permanently removed and the Move Reminder will continue to teach Pokémon moves for free.

Appearance

This is the appearance of the Heart Scale in the Sinnoh Underground.

Heart Scale


In the anime

A Heart Scale in the anime

Heart Scales first appeared in a fantasy in Partner Promises!, when Mallow mentioned how the jewelry shop "Alola Sunrise" makes its jewelry from Heart Scales and Corsola branches.

A Heart Scale made its first proper appearance in the Poké Problem extra scene of SM120, where Lana showed one to her classmates, jokingly claiming that it was a Kyogre scale.

A Heart Scale appeared in A Recipe for Success!, where Mallow obtained one from a group of Fearow that had come to visit Oranguru's place.

Trivia

  • Heart Scales are the shed scales of Luvdisc.
    • In Alola, they can be obtained at the Vast Poni Canyon trial site, implying they can also be the shed scales of the Jangmo-o evolutionary line. This is further supported by the fact that Jangmo-o has a heart-shaped scale on its head.
  • In Pokémon X and Y, the Heart Scale is incorrectly sorted in the Bag as an Evolution stone when the Bag is sorted by type, instead of as an exchangeable item.
