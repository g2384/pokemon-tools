The Fire Gem (Japanese: ほのおのジュエル Flame Jewel) is a type of held item introduced in Generation V. It is one of the Gems and is an unobtainable held item in Generation VI onwards.

Effect

Generation V

It increases the power of the holder's first Fire-type move by 50%, and is consumed after use.

Fling will fail if the user is holding a Fire Gem. Fire Pledge will never consume a Fire Gem.

Generation VI onward

It increases the power of the holder's first Fire-type move by 30%, and is consumed after use.
