The Silk Scarf (Japanese: シルクのスカーフ Silk Scarf) is a type of held item introduced in Generation III that boosts the power of Normal-type moves.

Effect

Generation III

Boosts the power of the holder's Normal-type moves by 10%. It also boosts the holder's confusion damage by 10%.

Generation IV

Boosts the power of the holder's Normal-type moves by 20%. It also boosts the holder's confusion damage by 20%.

Generation V onward

No longer boosts the holder's confusion damage.
