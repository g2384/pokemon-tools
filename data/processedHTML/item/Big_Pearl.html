In the Generation V games, the Big Pearl can be sold to the ore maniac inside the Icirrus City Pokémon Center for 7500.

In the Generation VIII games, the Big Pearl can be sold to the valuable item buyer in Stow-on-Side for 12,000.

Effect

The Big Pearl serves no practical purpose other than to be sold.
