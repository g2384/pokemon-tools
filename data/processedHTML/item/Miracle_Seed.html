The Miracle Seed (Japanese: きせきのタネ Miracle Seed) is a type of held item introduced in Generation II that boosts the power of Grass-type moves.

Effect

Generations II and III

Boosts the power of the holder's Grass-type moves by 10%.

Generation IV onward

Boosts the power of the holder's Grass-type moves by 20%.

In spin-off games

Miracle Seed in Pokémon: Magikarp Jump

Pokémon: Magikarp Jump

In Pokémon: Magikarp Jump, Miracle Seed is a Friendship Item that is received as a reward after beating the Luxury League. It summons Bulbasaur as a helper Pokémon.

In the anime

A Miracle Seed in the anime

In Currying Favor and Flavor!, Ash and Mallow discovered a Miracle Seed in a hollowed-out stump full of Berries in Lush Jungle during a quest Olivia sent them on to collect ingredients for the "Akala Curry".
