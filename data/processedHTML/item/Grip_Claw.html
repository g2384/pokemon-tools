The Grip Claw (Japanese: ねばりのかぎづめ Grip Claw) is a type of held item introduced in Generation IV.

Effect

Generation IV

When held by a Pokémon, it causes binding moves used by the holder to always continue for 5 turns.

Generation V onward

When held by a Pokémon, it causes binding moves used by the holder to always continue for 7 turns.
