Star Sweet (Japanese: スターアメざいく Star Amezaiku) is a type of held item introduced in Generation VIII. It is one of the sweets that can be used to evolve Milcery.

Price

Games Cost Sell price
SwSh N/A 250

Effect

Causes Milcery to evolve into Alcremie with a Star Sweet when the player does a spin while Milcery is holding it. This consumes the Star Sweet.

Games Description
SwSh A star-shaped sweet. When a Milcery holds this, it spins around happily.
