A Rowap Berry (Japanese: レンブのみ Renbu Fruit) is a type of Berry introduced in Generation IV. It is the final Berry in numerical order as of Generation V.

Growth and harvest

Generation IV

A Rowap Berry will mature from a planted seed to a full-grown, fruit-bearing tree in 96 hours, with 24 hours per stage. A Rowap tree will yield 1-5 Berries.

Uses

In-battle

When a Pokémon holds a Rowap Berry and is hit by a special attack, the attacker will lose 1/8 of its max HP.

Poffin cooking

At 100% performance, a Rowap Berry can produce a Level 41 (maybe higher) Sour-Spicy Poffin when cooking alone.
