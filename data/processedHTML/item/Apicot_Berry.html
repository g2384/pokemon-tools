Growth and harvest

Generation III

An Apicot Berry will mature from a planted seed to a full-grown, fruit-bearing tree in 96 hours, with 24 hours per stage. An Apicot tree will yield 1-2 Berries.

Generation IV

An Apicot Berry will mature from a planted seed to a full-grown, fruit-bearing tree in 96 hours, with 24 hours per stage. An Apicot tree will yield 1-5 Berries.

Generation VI

An Apicot Berry will mature from a planted seed to a full-grown, fruit-bearing tree in 96 hours, with 16 hours per stage. An Apicot tree will yield 1-10 Berries. During its growth, watering the plant will add 0.2 Berries to the final harvest, weeding it will add 0.1, and removing a pest will add 1.

Generation VII

An Apicot Berry will mature from a planted seed to a full-grown, fruit-bearing tree in 72 hours. An Apicot tree will yield 2-8 Berries.
