The Razor Claw (Japanese: するどいツメ Razor Claw) is a type of held item introduced in Generation IV. It allows Sneasel to evolve into Weavile or Sneasler, and also increases the holder's critical hit ratio.

Effect

In battle

Raises the critical hit ratio of the holder by one stage.

Outside battles

Causes Sneasel to evolve into Weavile when leveled up at night while holding it. This consumes the Razor Claw.

In Pokémon Legends: Arceus, the item causes Hisuian Sneasel to evolve into Sneasler when used during the day, and Johtonian Sneasel into Weavile when used at night. This consumes the Razor Claw.

In spin-off games

Pokémon Mystery Dungeon series

The Razor Claw appears in Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time, Explorers of Darkness, and Explorers of Sky.

Price

Games Cost Sell price
MDTDS 1000 1

Effect

If the player offers both a Lunar Ribbon and Razor Claw at Luminous Spring, Sneasel can be evolved into Weavile. This consumes the Lunar Ribbon and Razor Claw.

If thrown, it will deal 1 damage (2 if sticky). Using this item in a dungeon will consume it, with no effect.

Pokémon Conquest

Reason: Details on the non-evolution effect of the Razor Claw

In Pokémon Conquest, Sneasel evolves into Weavile if its link is improved while its Speed is 175 or higher and its Warrior is equipped with a Razor Claw. The Razor Claw also increases the probability of attacks hitting enemies' weak spots.

Razor Claw can be purchased from the traveling merchant for 5000 gold, and sold for 2500-3250 gold.
