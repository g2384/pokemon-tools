The Tiny Mushroom (Japanese: ちいさなキノコ Small Mushroom), formatted TinyMushroom prior to Generation VI, is a valuable item introduced in Generation II. In Pokémon FireRed and LeafGreen, two of these can be exchanged for a Move Reminder's service.

In the Generation V games, the TinyMushroom can be sold to the gourmet maniac on Route 5 for 500.

In the Generation VIII games, the Tiny Mushroom can be sold to the valuable item buyer in Stow-on-Side for 2,000.

Effect

In Pokémon FireRed and LeafGreen, two TinyMushrooms can be given to the Move Reminder on Two Island to relearn one move for one Pokémon.

In spin-off games

Pokémon Quest

A Tiny Mushroom in Pokémon Quest

A Tiny Mushroom is an ingredient that can be used in cooking to attract wild Pokémon in Pokémon Quest.

Description

Games Description
Quest Soft and small ingredients.

Acquisition

Tiny Mushroom is a possible ingredient that can be obtained by completing expeditions. It can also be obtained randomly by recycling Power Stones.

In the anime

Tiny Mushrooms in the anime

In Treasure Hunt, Akala Style!, Mallow found multiple Tiny Mushrooms during a Stoutland Search treasure hunt set up for Ash and his classmates by Olivia.

In Trade, Borrow, and Steal!, Kricketina Kylie used Tiny Mushroom powder as one of the ingredients for her "Kricketina Treats" mixture, made in an attempt to lure in a Pinsir for her to catch.
