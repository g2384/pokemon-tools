The Oval Stone (Japanese: まんまるいし Oval Stone) is a type of held item introduced in Generation IV. It is an Evolution item that allows Happiny to evolve into Chansey.

In the Generation V games, the Oval Stone can be sold to an item maniac inside the Icirrus City Pokémon Center for 1500.

Effect

Held item

Causes Happiny to evolve into Chansey when leveled up during the day while holding it. This consumes the Oval Stone.

In spin-off games

Pokémon Mystery Dungeon series

The Oval Stone appears in Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time, Explorers of Darkness, and Explorers of Sky.

Price

Games Cost Sell price
MDTDS 1000 1

Effect

If the player offers both a Sun Ribbon and Oval Stone at Luminous Spring, Happiny can be evolved into Chansey. This consumes the Sun Ribbon and Oval Stone.

If thrown, it will deal 1 damage (2 if sticky). Using this item in a dungeon will consume it, with no effect.

In the anime

An Oval Stone in the anime

In One Big Happiny Family!, Brock carved and made an Oval Stone for his newborn Happiny, which later helped her evolve into a Chansey in The Brockster Is In!.

In the manga

Pokémon Adventures

In the TCG

HeartGold & SoulSilver Chansey

An Oval Stone is featured in the artwork of the HeartGold & SoulSilver Chansey card.

Trivia

  • Prior to Generation VIII, the games' user interface handles the Oval Stone as if it were an Evolution stone.
    • The Oval Stone has the "Use" option when selected in the Bag, and when used it goes to the Pokémon party list like an Evolution stone, but all Pokémon are always listed as "Unable".
    • In Generation V, the Oval Stone is incorrectly categorized in the Bag as a general item rather than a held item.
    • When sorting items by type, the Oval Stone is sorted with Evolution stones rather than with other Evolution-inducing held items.
  • The Oval Stone is the only Evolution item with "Stone" in its English name that is not an Evolution stone.
