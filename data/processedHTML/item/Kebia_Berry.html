Growth and harvest

Generation IV

A Kebia Berry will mature from a planted seed to a full-grown, fruit-bearing tree in 72 hours, with 18 hours per stage. A Kebia tree will yield 1-5 Berries.

Generation VI

A Kebia Berry will mature from a planted seed to a full-grown, fruit-bearing tree in 48 hours, with 8 hours per stage. A Kebia tree will yield 3-20 Berries. During its growth, watering the plant will add 1 Berry to the final harvest, weeding it will add 0.5, and removing a pest will add 2.

Generation VII

A Kebia Berry will mature from a planted seed to a full-grown, fruit-bearing tree in 48 hours. A Kebia tree will yield 2-6 Berries.

Uses

Poffin Cooking

At 100% performance, a Kebia Berry can produce a Level 19 (maybe higher) Dry-Sour Poffin when cooking alone.
