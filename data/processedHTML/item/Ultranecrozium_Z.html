The Ultranecrozium Z (Japanese: ウルトラネクロＺ Ultranecro Z) is a type of Z-Crystal introduced in Pokémon Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon. It is a Pokémon-specific Z-Crystal associated with Necrozma and allows it to transform into Ultra Necrozma after fusing with Solgaleo or Lunala, and allows it to upgrade its Photon Geyser into the special Z-Move Light That Burns the Sky when it is Ultra Necrozma.

In battle

Allows Necrozma to transform into Ultra Necrozma after using Ultra Burst and upgrades its Photon Geyser into the special Z-Move Light That Burns the Sky.

A Pokémon transformed into Ultra Necrozma cannot use any other Z-Move than Light that Burns the Sky, even if holding the appropriate Z-Crystal.

Unaffected by item negating effects (such as Embargo and Klutz). Item manipulating effects (such as Trick and Fling) cannot remove a Z-Crystal from a Pokémon.

Rayquaza cannot Mega Evolve if it holds a Z-Crystal.

Outside of battle

A held Ultranecrozium Z is automatically removed if the Pokémon is traded between players.

An Ultranecrozium Z in Pokémon Adventures

Pokémon Adventures

Sun, Moon, Ultra Sun & Ultra Moon chapter

An image of an Ultranecrozium Z appeared in Transcend!! Ultra Necrozma!, where it was revealed to have been one of the aura-related items collected and researched by the Ultra Recon Squad. When held by Dulse, it had caused him to get drawn to Necrozma, who absorbed the item and used it to transform into Ultra Necrozma.
