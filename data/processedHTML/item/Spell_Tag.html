The Spell Tag (Japanese: のろいのおふだ Cursed O-fuda) is a type of held item introduced in Generation II that boosts the power of Ghost-type moves.

In spin-off games

Pokémon: Magikarp Jump

In Pokémon: Magikarp Jump, Spell Tag is a Friendship Item that can be purchased for 400. It summons Mimikyu as a helper Pokémon.
