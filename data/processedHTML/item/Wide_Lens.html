The Wide Lens (Japanese: こうかくレンズ Wide-Angle Lens) is a type of held item introduced in Generation IV that slightly boosts the holder's accuracy.

In the TCG

Wide Lens
Main article: Wide Lens (Roaring Skies 95)

Wide Lens was introduced as an Pokémon Tool card in the Pokémon Trading Card Game during the Japanese XY Era. It was first released in the Japanese Emerald Break expansion before debuting in English in the Roaring Skies expansion. When this Pokémon Tool card is attached to a Pokémon, that Pokémon's moves that target Benched Pokémon apply Weakness and Resistance, which is typically ignored for Benched Pokémon.
