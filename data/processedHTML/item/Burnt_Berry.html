The Burnt Berry (Japanese: やけたきのみ Burnt Fruit) is a type of Berry exclusive to the Generation II games. It cures a Pokémon of freeze.

From Generation III onward, the Burnt Berry's spiritual successor, the Aspear Berry, appears instead.

Effect

Held item

If the holder is frozen, it will consume its held Burnt Berry and be cured of freeze.

Bag item

When used from the Bag on a Pokémon, it cures that Pokémon of freeze. This consumes the Burnt Berry.
