The Red Scarf (Japanese: あかいバンダナ Red Bandanna) is a type of held item introduced in Generation III. It increases the holder's Coolness in a Pokémon Contest.

Effect

When held by a Pokémon, it raises its Coolness in the primary round of a Pokémon Contest, the Visual Competition of a Super Contest, and the Introduction Round of a Contest Spectacular.

In the manga

Scarves in Pokémon Adventures

Pokémon Adventures

Ruby & Sapphire chapter

Before competing in the Master Rank Contest, Ruby was given all five scarves, which he used to enhance the respective conditions of five of his Pokémon, leading all of them to win the Contest and fulfilling Ruby's part of the 80-day challenge with Sapphire. He used the Red Scarf to enhance Nana's Coolness.
