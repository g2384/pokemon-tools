The Magmarizer (Japanese: マグマブースター Magmabooster) is a type of held item introduced in Generation IV. It allows Magmar to evolve into Magmortar.

In spin-off games

Pokémon Mystery Dungeon series

The Magmarizer appears in Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time, Explorers of Darkness, and Explorers of Sky.

Price

Games Cost Sell price
MDTDS 1000 1

Effect

If the player offers both a Link Cable and Magmarizer at Luminous Spring, Magmar can be evolved into Magmortar. This consumes the Link Cable and Magmarizer.

If thrown, it will deal 1 damage (2 if sticky). Using this item in a dungeon will consume it, with no effect.
