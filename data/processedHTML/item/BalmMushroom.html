The Balm Mushroom (Japanese: かおるキノコ Fragrant Mushroom), formatted BalmMushroom prior to Generation VI, is a valuable item introduced in Generation V.

In the Generation V games, the BalmMushroom can be sold to the gourmet maniac on Route 5 for 25000.

In the Generation VIII games, the Balm Mushroom can be sold to the valuable item buyer in Stow-on-Side for 25,000.

Effect

The Balm Mushroom serves no practical purpose other than to be sold.

In spin-off games

Pokémon Quest

A Balm Mushroom in Pokémon Quest

A Balm Mushroom is an ingredient that can be used in cooking to attract wild Pokémon in Pokémon Quest.

Description

Games Description
Soft and precious ingredients.

Acquisition

Balm Mushroom is a possible ingredient that can be obtained by completing expeditions. It can also be obtained randomly by recycling Power Stones.
