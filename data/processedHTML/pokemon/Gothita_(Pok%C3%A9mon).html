Gothita (Japanese: ゴチム Gothimu) is a Psychic-type Pokémon introduced in Generation V.

It evolves into Gothorita starting at level 32, which evolves into Gothitelle starting at level 41.

Biology

Gothita is a small, humanoid Pokémon. A small tuft of hair sticks up from the top of its head, and it has two white, bow-shaped feelers on each side of its purple head and a third bow-shaped feeler on its neck. These ribbon-like feelers are used to amplify Gothita's psychic power. It has two large blue eyes with three eyelashes on each and small red lips. Gothita intently observes those around it, becoming so focused, it is unaware of attacks. Its torso is black with a white zigzagging line running horizontally through the middle. Its arms and legs are short and stubby with no visible digits.

In the anime

Gothita in the anime

Major appearances

Gothita debuted in Where Did You Go, Audino?, under the ownership of Christie. It was used to help out on the disappearance of Audino. After two Audino destroyed a bridge with Psychic so the group could not follow them, Christie commanded Gothita to use Telekinesis to get them across the river. It was then used to battle Team Rocket by using Psyshock on Woobat.

In PK22, a wild Gothita, Drilbur, and Darumaka were angry at Oshawott and Tepig, who had eaten the apples that they had collected.

In Scraggy and the Demanding Gothita!, Katharine's Gothita developed a crush on Scraggy and hoped he would join Katharine's team. However, Snivy defeated her in a battle, making her retreat.

In Eevee & Friends, a Gothita and its evolved forms were friends with an Umbreon living with an Eevee and the rest of the Eeveelutions.

In Pikachu and the Pokémon Music Squad, a Gothita was living in a forest.

In A Dancing Debut!, Nini used a Gothita in the Theme Performance of the Couriway Showcase alongside Nini's Smoochum and Farfetch'd. It helped Nini prepare a Poké Puff, advancing her to the Freestyle Performance. There, it delivered a performance along with its teammates, but it did not receive enough votes to win.

Trivia

  • Gothita can be seen as a parallel to Solosis. Both are Generation V Psychic-type Pokémon evolving once at level 32 and then again at level 41. Gothita is version-exclusive to Pokémon Black, Pokémon Black 2, and Pokémon Sword, while Solosis is version-exclusive to Pokémon White, Pokémon White 2, and Pokémon Shield. They both have the same base experience and catch rates, gain new moves at similar levels and have base stat totals of 290.

Origin

Gothita seems to be based on a child dressed in Gothic lolita fashion.

Name origin

Gothita may be a combination of gothic and lolita.

Gothimu may be a combination of gothic and 予知夢 yochimu (precognitive dream) or 見る miru (to observe).
