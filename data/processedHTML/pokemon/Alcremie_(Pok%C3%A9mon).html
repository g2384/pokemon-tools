Alcremie (Japanese: マホイップ Mawhip) is a Fairy-type Pokémon introduced in Generation VIII.

It evolves from Milcery while holding a Sweet when its Trainer spins and strikes a pose. The form it takes upon evolving depends on the time of the day, the length of the spin, and whether the Trainer was spinning clockwise or counterclockwise. Its appearance is further determined by the type of Sweet that is held.

  • Milcery evolves into Vanilla Cream Alcremie after spinning clockwise for less than 5 seconds during the day.
  • Milcery evolves into Ruby Cream Alcremie after spinning counterclockwise for less than 5 seconds during the day.
  • Milcery evolves into Matcha Cream Alcremie after spinning clockwise for less than 5 seconds at night.
  • Milcery evolves into Mint Cream Alcremie after spinning counterclockwise for more than 5 seconds at night.
  • Milcery evolves into Lemon Cream Alcremie after spinning clockwise for more than 5 seconds at night.
  • Milcery evolves into Salted Cream Alcremie after spinning counterclockwise for less than 5 seconds at night.
  • Milcery evolves into Ruby Swirl Alcremie after spinning counterclockwise for more than 5 seconds during the day.
  • Milcery evolves into Caramel Swirl Alcremie after spinning clockwise for more than 5 seconds during the day.
  • Milcery evolves into Rainbow Swirl Alcremie after spinning counterclockwise for more than 10 seconds between 7:00 PM and 7:59 PM in-game time.

Alcremie has a Gigantamax form which is the same regardless of Alcremie's form.

Biology

Alcremie is a vaguely anthropoid Pokémon with a body made of whipped cream. Its head resembles several clumps of whipped cream styled like hair, with a large crowning blob flanked by two loop-like "bangs." Each of these bangs is topped with the Sweet Alcremie was given to hold as a Milcery when evolving. Alcremie's lower body is similarly composed of two dollops of whipped cream resembling a robe or a dress. It has two stout arms with three fingers each extending between the dollops of its body.

Alcremie's flavor and color are wildly variable and dependent on the time of day, direction, and duration for which it was swirled while it was a Milcery. An Alcremie's flavor affects the color of its body, which can be any of several pastel shades. Its eye color matches the color of its sweet. The content of the cream can become richer and sweeter thanks to its emotion.

In its Gigantamax form, Alcremie's body resembles a towering, five-tiered cake. The apical tier of the cake resembles Alcremie's regular body, though it's wearing a bow and its whipped-cream "hair" is styled into a flamboyant swoop. Each layer of the cake features a variety of sweets that can be used to evolve Milcery. The bottom tier of its body is frosted yellow and adorned with an alternating row of star sweets and dotted frosting. The second tier is frosted pink and features a magenta bow topped with a clover sweet. The third tier is frosted blue and encircled with a row of flower sweets featuring a berry sweet arch. The fourth tier is frosted pink and encircled with love sweets and three red clouds, with the central sweet being larger than the rest. Lumps of whipped cream surround Gigantamax Alcremie's body between each of its tiers. Gigantamax Aclcremie's cream is said to be endlessly pouring.

When a trainer gains Alcremie's trust, Alcremie will treat them to Berries with cream. Due to the cream it makes being very delicious, many pastry chefs seek to have Alcremie as a partner. If attacked, it will throw a sweet-scented cream to distract or blind the opponent in order to escape. The cream's effect is powerful enough to make an opponent soothed and lose the will to battle should they eat the cream.[1]

When the cream gets hit by a strong impact, the cream hardens depending on the blow. This allows it to be resistant to all physical attacks while the giant berry decorations cannot be scratched due to them being as hard as diamond. When Gigantamax Alcremie unleashes its super high-calorie missiles, each filled with 100,000 kilocalories, it will cause the opponent who touches the cream to be filled with energy and euphoria, however the opponent will then fall into a state of complete confusion along with the other two effects.[2]

Alcremie is the only known Pokémon capable of using the move Decorate and the exclusive G-Max Move G-Max Finale.

Major appearances

Multiple Alcremie made their main series debut in The Sweet Taste of Battle!, with most of them belonging to Opal, one belonging to a pâtissier, and one evolving from Goh's newly caught Milcery. One of Opal's Alcremie was shown to be capable of Gigantamaxing.

Trivia

  • Alcremie has the most different recognized forms of all Pokémon, with 64.
    • As a result, Alcremie also has the most Pokédex entries for a single Pokémon.
  • Alcremie is tied with Minior as being under the most Pokédex color groupings, with a total of six.

Origin

Alcremie appears to be based on whipped cream, or eton mess, a British dessert made of fruit, meringue, and whipped cream. Its Gigantamax form may be based on a wedding cake.

Name origin

Alcremie may be a combination of alchemy or à la mode (a dish topped with ice cream), and cream or creamy.

Mawhip may be a combination of 魔法 mahō (magic) and ホイップ whip (whipped cream).
